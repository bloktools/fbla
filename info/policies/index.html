Policies

1. General Conduct

Visitors are expected to behave in a respectful and responsible manner at all times.

Disruptive behavior, harassment, profanity, or aggression of any kind is not tolerated.

All patrons must comply with the requests of staff, security, and event organizers.

2. Ticketing and Entry

Tickets must be purchased through official channels and presented at the gate.

Children under 12 years must be accompanied by an adult.

Re-entry is not permitted unless otherwise specified for individual events.

3. Bag Policy

Only clear bags and small purses, no larger than 6" x 9" are permitted.

Backpacks, over-sized bags and luggage are not allowed into the venue.

All bags may be subject to search before entry.

4. Prohibited Items

Outside food and beverages, except for medical or infant needs.

Weapons, fireworks, and illegal substances.

Noise-makers, laser pointers, and oversized signs.

Drones or other aerial devices.

5. Food and Beverage

Concessions are available inside the arena.

Service of alcohol will only be provided to guests 21 years of age or older who provide a valid ID.

Outside food or drink is not permitted except for medical purposes.

6. Photography and Recording

Flash photography and professional recording equipment are not permitted.

Photography for private use is permitted unless otherwise specified by event organizers.

Recording or live streaming events in any way is not allowed without prior authorization.

7. Accessibility

Wheelchair accessibility is provided throughout the arena.

Guests with special needs should contact the venue in advance.

Service animals are welcome, but no other animals are permitted.

8. Parking and Transportation

On-site parking is available for a fee. Carpooling is encouraged.

Any vehicle parked in an unauthorized area will be towed at the owner's expense.

Ride-share pickup and drop-off zones are located near the main entrance.

9. Lost and Found

Items found during events will be held at the Guest Services desk.

Unclaimed items will be stored for 30 days before donation.

10. Weather Policy

Outdoor events may be delayed or rescheduled in case of severe weather.

Refunds or exchanges will only be available in the case of event cancellation.

11. Refund and Cancellation Policy

Tickets are only refundable with an arena cancellation of the event.

In the event of a reschedule, tickets will remain valid for the new date.

12. Emergencies

In case of an emergency, follow instructions from staff and security.

Emergency exits are clearly marked throughout the arena.

13. Community Standards

The arena is committed to maintaining a safe and respectful environment.

Discrimination, or hateful behavior of any kind, will not be tolerated.
