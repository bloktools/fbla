Policies

1. General Conduct

Visitors are to conduct themselves in a respectful and responsible manner at all times.

Disruptive behavior, including harassment, profanity, or aggression, is prohibited.

All guests must follow the instructions of staff, security, and event organizers.

2. Ticketing and Entry

Tickets must be purchased through official channels and presented at the gate.

Children under 12 must be accompanied by an adult.

Re-entry is not allowed unless otherwise stated for specific events.

3. Bag Policy

Only clear bags and small purses (no larger than 6” x 9”) are permitted.

Backpacks, oversized bags, and luggage are prohibited.

All bags are subject to search upon entry.

4. Prohibited Items

Outside food and beverages, except for medical or infant needs.

Weapons, fireworks, and illegal substances.

Noise-makers, laser pointers, and oversized signs.

Drones or other aerial devices.

5. Food and Beverage

Concessions are available inside the arena.

Alcohol will only be served to individuals 21 years or older with a valid ID.

Outside food or beverages are not allowed unless medically necessary.

6. Photography and Recording

Flash photography and professional recording equipment are not permitted.

Photography for personal use is allowed unless restricted by event organizers.

Recording or live streaming events without prior approval is prohibited.

7. Accessibility

The arena is wheelchair accessible, with designated seating areas.

Guests requiring special assistance should contact the venue in advance.

Service animals are welcome, but no other animals are allowed.

8. Parking and Transportation

Parking is available on-site for a fee. Carpooling is encouraged.

Vehicles parked in unauthorized areas will be towed at the owner's expense.

Ride-share pickup and drop-off zones are located near the main entrance.

9. Lost and Found

Items found during events will be held at the Guest Services desk.

Unclaimed items will be stored for 30 days before donation.

10. Weather Policy

Outdoor events may be delayed or rescheduled in case of severe weather.

Refunds or exchanges will be offered only if the event is canceled.

11. Refund and Cancellation Policy

Tickets are only refundable if the arena cancels the event.

For rescheduled events, tickets will remain valid for the new date.

12. Emergencies

In case of an emergency, follow instructions from staff and security.

Emergency exits are clearly marked throughout the arena.

13. Community Standards

The arena is committed to fostering an inclusive and safe environment.

Discrimination or hateful behavior of any kind will not be tolerated.
